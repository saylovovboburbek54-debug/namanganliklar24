On May 19, a videoconference meeting was held to discuss additional measures to support entrepreneurship.

According to the presidential press service, the pandemic in Uzbekistan will increase the number of poor and unemployed, while family incomes will decline. Additional measures and priorities in this area were discussed at the meeting.

First of all, serious attention is paid to ensuring guarantees of the rights of entrepreneurs and the inviolability of their property.

"I am warning all khokims. If the property of even one entrepreneur is seized illegally, the khokim will not get away with just resignation, but will also be held accountable before the law," Shavkat Mirziyoyev emphasized.

Competent agencies have been instructed to strengthen penalties for violating property rights and to ensure the full enforcement of court decisions on compensation for entrepreneurs' property.

In accordance with the President's instructions, business activity in each district and city will be analyzed on a monthly basis.
